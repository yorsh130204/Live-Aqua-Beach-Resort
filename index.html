About Live Aqua Beach Resort Cancún

Welcome to Live Aqua Beach Resort Cancún! Situated in the heart of the stunning Cancún coastline, our resort offers an unparalleled experience of luxury and relaxation. Immerse yourself in our breathtaking ocean views, indulge in world-class amenities, and savor the finest dining options, creating unforgettable memories during your stay.

Why Live Aqua Beach Resort Cancún?

Oceanfront Bar

Indulge in our oceanfront bar, perfect for enjoying moments with your partner or friends while overlooking the stunning sea. Listen to live music, dance, and savor our signature drinks, including the classic "Margarita" or the local favorite, Pacific Beer.

More Information
Hidden Garden Restaurant

Experience the Hidden Garden Restaurant with breathtaking sea views, open from 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Indulge in a variety of delightful Mexican and international dishes a la carte. Specializing in fine cuts and seafood, we also offer a Sunday buffet and daily room service until 9:30 p.m.

More Information
Amenities

Enjoy our resort amenities, including two swimming pools with waterfalls and jacuzzis, wireless internet, 24-hour security, room service, free parking, laundry service ($), sport fishing reservations, doctor ($), babysitting service upon request ($), jewelry store, tobacco shop, event room, and meeting facilities.
